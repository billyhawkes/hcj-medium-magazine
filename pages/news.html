Australia Floods: Over 40,000 People Evacuated.

By Billy Hawkes

·

March 24

Floods washing away homes, livestock, and roads in Australia.

Many days of terrential rain are the cause of Australia's large flooding. Waterfalls form on this beautiful countries Uluru rocks. Australias worst flooding, and all this after a long year of bush fires.

What kind of rain?

This flooding has gone into the range of nearly 40 inches of rain in one poor area of South Wales. Another area getting around 20 inches in one single day. This type of rain is submerging houses and leaving farm and otherwise animals stranded as the citizens are under evacuation orders.

Sadly, this wednesday, two bodies were found. One 25 year old man, had been submerged in his car while calling for help when the connection was cut off.

Ulura Waterfalls

Rainwater on the Ulura falls has built up leaving a beautiful waterfall that has many citizens swarming

The Warragamba Dam has been overflowing, an this overflow is at around the same amount of water as the citys harbor
