Large cargo ship fully blocking the Suez Canal

By Billy Hawkes

·

March 25

Owner of the cargo ship has Apoligized.

The owner Shoei Kisen Kaisha has apoligized for blocking the canal. This ship has been stuck since Tuesday with over 150 other ships just waiting for passage.

Experts are working at the problem, but have failed their latest attempt just Today.

Effects On Trade.

The Suez Canal is a heavily used waterway connecting the Red and Mediterranean Seal. A lot of the boats that use this cannal are for business. Around 12% of global trade happens to use this cannal.

With such important trade, people have to use an alternative route that can take up to two weeks longer to travel. This is a large problem, and such the owner and others, have claims of millions for the delays and costs for the other business on the canal
