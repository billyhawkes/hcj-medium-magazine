'The Irregulars', Netflix's new Sherlock Holmes reboot

By Billy Hawkes

·

March 26

Sherlock Holmes Passed Forward

The widely known Sherlock Holmes has been passed down to a younger cast and audience. The show 'The Irregulars' is based on a group from the origional Sherlock Holmes series. This group goes by the name, Baker Street Irregulars, hence the show title.

A Supernatural Show

This show isn't your typical, solving cases duo, but instead a solving supernatural mysteries team of youngsters. This team is run by a 17 year old girl named Bea, and live in the streets of London.

How are the Reviews?

While only time can tell how well this show is taken, there is some early reviews. Rottentomatoes with a tomatometer of 80% and a 70% from top critics. These reviews seem quite good for a teen drama show such as this. Where the scores fail is with IMDB, giving it a 4.0 so far.

We can only keep an eye out and hope the best for the future of this show, and if you do pick it up for your nights, I hope you enjoy.
